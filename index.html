The Sustainable Form of Agriculture is Entrepreneurship.

What We Do

We believe that ultimately the only sustainable form of Agriculture is entrepreneurship, so we seek to establish agricultural based businesses, enable the development of agricultural entrepreneurship, and promote and facilitate education in agricultural practices that will expand African farmers passed subsistence or sub-subsistence farming to a production system that creates sustainable commerce thereby producing jobs, raising their standard of living, and creating opportunity and social benefit (the ability to pay for school, medical expenses, or start other businesses).
